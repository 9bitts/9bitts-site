About

A small home
in the universe.

9bitts began as a curation of melodic techno and deep house. It has grown into something wider — a slow exploration of what music carries when it stops being just sound. Art, philosophy, and the quiet work of paying attention.

Music

Weekly sets curated from Beatport, mixed with intention. Melodic techno, deep house, atmospheric sound — chosen one track at a time for what they carry.

Art

Visual work that breathes with the sound. Each carousel and post is composed with the same attention you'd give a song — image, text, and silence working together.

Depth

Reflections drawn from thinkers who looked closely — Jung, Marcus Aurelius, da Vinci, Tarkovsky. Not as quotes to consume, but as questions to sit with.

Technology

Built on a custom automation pipeline — so the craft happens where it matters: selecting the music, finding the words, sitting with the work.
