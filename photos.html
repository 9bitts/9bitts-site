Visual archive

The floor.
The booth.
The light.

Electronic music lives in the body before it lives anywhere else. This is where we keep what the camera caught — moments from events, clubs, and nights that mattered.

What we're looking for

Three kinds of moment.

Every good photo from an electronic music event captures one of three things — or all three at once.

Examples below are from Unsplash — free photographers who documented the scene.

01 — The floor

Crowd energy

People moving, hands up, eyes closed. The collective body of a dance floor at its peak — or its quietest moment.

02 — The booth

The DJ at work

Hands on equipment, focused face, the geometry of a mixer. Performance shots from behind or beside the decks.

03 — The light

Atmosphere & space

Lasers, strobes, fog, shadows. The visual texture of a night — stage shots, wide angles, abstract light.

For photographers

Your lens.
Our archive.

9bitts is building a visual record of electronic music culture. If you've photographed a rave, a club night, a festival — small or large — we want to see your work. Every photo is credited. Every photographer is linked.

01

Select 3–5 photos

JPG or PNG, minimum 1800px on the longest side. Colour or black and white.

02

Send by email

Include the event name, location, and your portfolio or social link for credit.

03

We publish with credit

Your name and link appear on every photo. You keep full rights to your work.
